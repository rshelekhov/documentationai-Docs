## Overview

Keep your documentation tidy using folders and tags.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create folders">
    Click "Add Folder" in your project to group related pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move pages">
    Drag and drop pages into folders as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tags">
    Edit a page, add keywords in the Tags section to find content easily.
  </Step>
</Steps>
