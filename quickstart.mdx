## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for social login

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up takes under 2 minutes. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to [https://app.rshelekhov.com](https://app.rshelekhov.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and agree to the terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from `no-reply@rshelekhov.com` and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a business email for team collaboration features.
</Callout>

## Initial Setup

After verification, complete your profile setup.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Login" icon="mail">

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Social Login" icon="github">

Customize your preferences:

- Set your default theme (light/dark)
- Enable real-time collaboration
